Billing & Plans

PhotoHouse offers three tiers — Free, Pro, and Studio — with limits managed by the platform administrator. Billing is handled by Stripe; your card details are never stored on the PhotoHouse server.

Limits are admin-configurable

Exact storage sizes, event limits, and feature availability are set by the platform administrator in /admin/plans and /admin/settings. The table above shows defaults.

Upgrading Your Plan

  1. Navigate to the Pricing page

    Go to /pricing to see all available plans and their current prices.

  2. Click "Get started" on your chosen plan

    You'll be redirected to Stripe Checkout where you enter your card details.

  3. Complete payment

    After a successful payment Stripe sends a webhook and your account is upgraded instantly.

Managing Your Subscription

Go to Dashboard → Billing to view your current plan, next billing date, and payment method. Click Manage billing to open the Stripe Customer Portal where you can:

  • Update your credit card
  • Download invoices
  • Switch between monthly and annual billing
  • Cancel your subscription

Invoice History

Past invoices are fetched from Stripe and displayed on the Billing page. Each row shows the date, description, amount, status, and a link to download the PDF invoice.

Cancellation

Cancel at any time from the Stripe Customer Portal. You retain access to your current plan's features until the end of the billing period. After that, your account reverts to Free. Events and photos are not deleted — but you'll be unable to create new events beyond the Free limit until you upgrade again.

⚠️
Storage over limit after downgrade

If your stored photos exceed the Free plan's 1 GB limit after downgrading, new uploads will be blocked. You won't lose existing photos, but you'll need to delete some or upgrade to continue uploading.
