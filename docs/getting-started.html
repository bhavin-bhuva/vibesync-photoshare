Getting Started

Welcome to PhotoHouse — a client gallery platform built for professional photographers. This guide walks you through creating your account, setting up your studio, and delivering your first gallery to a client.

Creating Your Account

Navigate to the PhotoHouse registration page and fill in your details. All new accounts start on the Free plan — no credit card required.

  1. Open the registration page

    Go to /register and enter your full name, email address, and a secure password.

  2. Verify your email

    Check your inbox for a verification link. Click it to activate your account.

  3. Log in and explore the dashboard

    Use the credentials you just created to log in. You'll land on the events dashboard.

Setting Up Your Studio Profile

Before sharing galleries with clients, personalise your studio so your branding appears on every gallery page.

Personal information

Go to Profile → Personal Info to update your display name and change your password.

Studio branding

Under Profile → Studio Branding, you can set:

  • Studio name — shown in gallery headers and notification emails.
  • Logo — upload a square PNG or JPG (recommended 400×400 px). Appears in the gallery hero and watermarks.
  • Tagline — a short line shown below your studio name on galleries.
  • Brand colour — hex colour used as the gallery hero background fallback.
  • Contact details — website, phone, and address displayed in gallery footers.
💡
Tip

Upload a transparent-background logo for the best result on both light and dark gallery heroes.

Creating Your First Event

An event is a collection of photos for a single shoot — a wedding, a headshot session, a family portrait. Each event gets its own private gallery link.

  1. Click "+ New Event" on the dashboard

    A modal appears. Enter the event name and shoot date.

  2. Upload your photos

    Click Upload Photos inside the event. Drag files onto the upload zone or click to browse. PhotoHouse queues and uploads in parallel.

  3. Share the gallery with your client

    Click Share Event, choose a PIN or password, and copy the link to send to your client.

Dashboard Overview

The dashboard gives you a quick view of your studio:

  • Stats bar — total events, photos, active share links, and storage used.
  • New selections banner — appears when a client has submitted a photo selection.
  • Event grid — each card shows the cover photo, date, group count, and link count.
ℹ️
Free plan limits

Free accounts can create up to 3 events and 1 GB of storage. Upgrade to Pro or Studio for more. See Billing & Plans.
